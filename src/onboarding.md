# Employee Onboarding Procedure

**Owner:** {{hr_lead}}
**Applies to:** All new employees at {{company_name}}
**Last Reviewed:** {{last_reviewed}}

---

## Purpose

This procedure ensures every new employee joining {{company_name}} receives a consistent, thorough onboarding experience and is set up for success from day one.

---

## Scope

This procedure applies to all full-time, part-time, and contract employees joining {{company_name}} at any location, including remote workers.

---

## Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| HR Lead ({{hr_lead}}) | Coordinate end-to-end onboarding process |
| IT Lead ({{it_lead}}) | Provision equipment and system access |
| Line Manager | Assign buddy, conduct 30/60/90 day check-ins |
| New Employee | Complete required training within timelines |

---

## Procedure

### Before Start Date (HR — {{hr_lead}})

1. Send offer letter and contract for signature
2. Collect right-to-work documentation
3. Add employee to HR system with start date
4. Notify IT Lead ({{it_lead}}) to prepare equipment and accounts
5. Send welcome email including:
   - Start date, time, and location (or remote setup instructions)
   - First day agenda
   - First week schedule
   - Contact for questions: {{compliance_officer_email}}

### Before Start Date (IT — {{it_lead}})

1. Provision laptop or workstation
2. Create company email account (`firstname.lastname@{{company_website | trimPrefix "https://"}}`)
3. Grant access to core systems based on role profile
4. Prepare IT welcome guide with credentials and setup steps

### Day 1

1. Welcome meeting with HR ({{hr_lead}})
2. Office/remote environment tour
3. Introduction to team and assigned buddy
4. IT setup walkthrough ({{it_lead}} or delegate)
5. Review and sign {{company_name}} policies:
   - Code of Conduct
   - Data Protection Policy
   - IT Acceptable Use Policy

### Week 1

1. Complete mandatory training modules (see Training Register)
2. 1:1 with line manager — set 30/60/90 day goals
3. Introduction meetings with key stakeholders

### 30 / 60 / 90 Day Check-ins

Line manager conducts structured check-ins at 30, 60, and 90 days to review:
- Progress against goals
- Any blockers or support needed
- Feedback on onboarding experience
