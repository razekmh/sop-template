# Data Protection & Security Policy

**Owner:** {{compliance_officer}}
**Applies to:** All staff at {{company_name}}
**Last Reviewed:** {{last_reviewed}}

---

## Purpose

{{company_name}} is committed to protecting personal data and confidential information in accordance with applicable data protection law. This policy sets out the obligations of all staff and how {{company_short}} manages data securely.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who process data on behalf of {{company_name}}.

---

## Data Protection Principles

All personal data processed by {{company_name}} must be:

1. **Processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently**
2. **Collected for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes**
3. **Adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary**
4. **Accurate and kept up to date**
5. **Retained only as long as necessary**
6. **Processed securely**

## Data Classification

{{company_name}} classifies data into three tiers:

| Classification | Description | Examples |
|---------------|-------------|---------|
| **Public** | May be shared externally | Marketing materials, published reports |
| **Internal** | For {{company_short}} staff only | Internal memos, meeting notes |
| **Confidential** | Restricted access, must be encrypted | Personal data, financial records, contracts |

---

## Security Controls

### Access Control
- All systems require unique user credentials — shared passwords are prohibited
- Access is granted on a least-privilege basis by {{it_lead}}
- Leavers have all access revoked on their last working day

### Device Security
- All company devices must have full-disk encryption enabled
- Screen lock activates after 5 minutes of inactivity
- Personal devices may only access company data via approved MDM or VPN

### Passwords
- Minimum 12 characters, mixing letters, numbers, and symbols
- Passwords must not be reused across systems
- A company-approved password manager is provided by {{it_lead}}

### Data Transfer
- Confidential data must only be transferred via encrypted channels
- External transfer of confidential data requires prior approval from {{compliance_officer}}

---

## Data Breach Response

Any suspected data breach must be reported **immediately** to:

- **{{compliance_officer}}** — {{compliance_officer_email}}
- **{{it_lead}}** (IT containment)

{{company_name}} will assess and, where required by law, notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of a breach.
